1. Bikeshare User Behavior & Market Strategy

Comprehensive case study analyzing bikeshare data to identify differences in user behavior between casual riders and subscription members. The final deliverable focused on data-driven recommendations for service expansion and subscription optimization for a fictional company.

Key Insights: Identified optimal neighborhoods for expansion and proposed multiple promising subscription models, directly answering a core business question.

2. Small Business Financial Health Reconciliation

A real-world project analyzing raw business bank statements for a small company in the food/beverage industry, to reconcile and categorize all revenue and expense streams. Focus was on rigorous data reconciliation and creating a transparent financial view for business oversight.

Key Insights: Uncovered evidence that over 20% of monthly spending was incorrectly categorized, leading to a reconciled budget and improved financial transparency.

View Reconciled Report (PDF) GitHub Repository

Tools Used: Google Sheets, Data Reconciliation, ETL

3. Fitness Tracker Data: Behavior and Correlation

Exploratory analysis of anonymous user fitness data, utilizing SQL to join metrics and Tableau to visualize relationships between user activity, sleep, and heart rate.

Key Insights: Established a strong correlation between sleep duration and daily activity levels, providing a foundation for targeted wellness program recommendations.

Tools Used: PostgreSQL, Tableau (Gantt Charts, Scatterplots), Google Sheets
